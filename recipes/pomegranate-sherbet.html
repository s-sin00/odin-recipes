Pomegranate Sherbet

Description

A refreshing dessert made of pomegranate juice, perfect for making ahead of time.

Ingredients

  • 3 cups unsweetened pomegranate juice
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 3 tablespoons lemon juice
  • 1 dash salt
  • 1/4 cup cold water
  • 1 (.25 ounce) envelope unflavored gelatin
  • 1 cup chilled whipping cream

Steps

  1. Stir together pomegranate juice, sugar, lemon juice, and salt in a large bowl. Chill, covered, 30 minutes.
  2. Put the cold water in a saucepan and sprinkle with gelatin; let stand 1 minute. Cook, stirring, over low heat just until gelatin dissolves, about 2 minutes. Stir into pomegranate mixture.
  3. Add cream; beat with an electric hand mixer at medium speed until mixture is foamy, about 3 minutes.
  4. Freeze in an ice cream maker according to manufacturer's directions until sherbet reaches soft-serve consistency, 1 to 1 1/2 hours.
  5. Transfer to a 1- or 2-qt. freezer-safe container. Put a piece of plastic wrap directly on the surface; cover and freeze at least 2 hours or up to 1 month.
